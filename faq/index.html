What it is

What is TwoUs?

A daily ritual for two partners: one shared question a day, answered independently, revealed only after you've both answered — plus a reflection step, small appreciations, and a shared timeline.

What is it not?

Not a chat app, not social media, not a therapist or AI companion. No feed, no likes, no follower counts.

Why only one question a day?

The constraint is the point. One real question creates one real conversation, instead of another feed to scroll and ignore.

Why can't I chat in it?

WhatsApp already exists. TwoUs is deliberately not messaging — it's a calm space for reflection and discovery.

Why does my partner's answer stay hidden until I answer?

So neither of you is influenced by the other, and no one feels put on the spot. You both answer honestly in private, then discover each other's answers in the same moment.

How it works

How do we pair up?

Sign up → verify the email code → connect with your partner's 8-character invite code (tap their invite link, or enter the code on the sign-up screen).

How long does it take each day?

Usually 2–5 minutes. A one-sentence answer is completely fine.

When does a new question appear?

A fresh question each day at your local midnight. A gentle evening reminder if you haven't answered — but you can answer any time.

Can my partner see my answer immediately?

No. Both of you must answer before either answer is revealed.

What if my partner hasn't answered yet?

Your answer stays private until they do. You'll see "Your answer is in ✓", and you'll be notified the moment they answer.

What is the reflection section?

After the reveal, you each answer one more thing — "What surprised you most about their answer?" — revealed once you've both written it. Most couples say this is where the real conversation starts.

What is Cherish?

A place to send one small, specific thank-you ("I appreciated that you…"), up to once a day. They build into a shared archive of good moments.

Where are my past answers?

In the Timeline tab — every question you've both answered, with both answers and your reflections.

Can I answer with a photo or voice note?

Yes — text, a photo, or a voice note (up to ~2 minutes).

Privacy & trust

Can the creators read my answers?

No. Your answers, reflections and appreciations are for you and your partner only. To understand whether the app works we look only at usage signals — counts and timestamps (e.g. whether both of you answered) — never the content of what you wrote.

Do you sell my data?

No. We don't sell your data and there is no advertising.

Do you use my answers to train AI?

No. Your answers are not used to train AI models.

Is it end-to-end encrypted?

Not yet — being upfront. Your content is stored on secure infrastructure (encrypted in transit and at rest; photos/voice behind access-controlled links) under the "we don't read your answers" rule above. Stronger encryption is on our roadmap. Full policy: privacy policy.

Account & data

What if we stop using the app?

Nothing is lost — your memories stay in the Timeline whenever you come back.

What if we disconnect?

The relationship is archived: no new questions, but your shared memories remain viewable (read-only).

What if I delete my account?

Your personal info is removed/anonymised (your name shows as "Former Partner"); shared memories may remain anonymised so your partner's timeline isn't broken.

Practical

I didn't get the email code.

Check spam, then tap "Resend code" (60-second cooldown).

Today's question won't load / it's slow the first time.

The first open after a while can take ~30s to wake the server — it retries on its own. If still blank, pull down to refresh.

I'm not getting notifications.

Allow notifications for TwoUs in your phone settings and make sure you're logged in.

Is it on the Play Store / App Store?

Not during the pilot — it's an Android app from twous.ajithkumarr.com/download. iPhone support comes later.
