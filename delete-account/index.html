Aham - Delete Your Account

Last updated: 19 June 2026

Aham (developed by Ajith Kumar) lets you permanently delete your account and the personal data associated with it. You can do this yourself inside the app, or request it from us by email - both options are described below.

Delete your account from inside the app

  1. Open Aham and go to the Us tab, then open Profile.
  2. Tap Account & Privacy.
  3. Tap Delete my account and confirm.

This takes effect immediately and signs you out on all devices.

Request deletion by email

If you can no longer sign in, email us at twousapp@gmail.com from the email address on your account, with the subject “Aham account deletion request”. We will verify ownership and delete your account within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account profile: name, email address, password, and profile photo.
  • Your push-notification token and your encryption public key.
  • Your relationship details (start date, relationship type, long-distance flag, time-zone offset).
  • Your personal content - answers, reflections, appreciation notes, photos, and voice notes - is anonymized and disconnected from your identity. Because this content is end-to-end encrypted and shared within a couple, any items your partner also took part in are retained for your partner's shared timeline, but are no longer linked to you or readable as yours.

What may be retained, and for how long

  • Basic operational and security logs (for example, request logs needed to run and protect the service) may be retained for up to 90 days, then deleted.
  • Limited records may be kept longer only where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or prevent fraud and abuse.

Contact

Questions about deleting your data? Email twousapp@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
