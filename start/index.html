Before you start — read this once, together if you can.

The goal is not to answer perfectly.
The goal is to answer honestly.

There are no right answers here. No one is grading you — your partner isn't either. A single true sentence is worth more than a paragraph you polished to sound good.

If a question makes you pause, that pause is the point. Say the real thing. Curiosity matters more than performance — you're not trying to impress each other, you're trying to notice each other.

  • Short is fine. One line. A photo. A ten-second voice note.
  • Slow is fine. This isn't a chat — there's no rush to reply.
  • Missing a day is fine. No streak to break, no penalty. Just come back.
  • You answer in private. Your partner can't see your answer until they've answered too.

Open the app, read today's question, and tell each other something true.
The rest takes care of itself.
